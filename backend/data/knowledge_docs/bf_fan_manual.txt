BF FAN 12 — BLAST FURNACE COOLING FAN

NORMAL OPERATING PARAMETERS:
- Vibration (RMS): 2.0–2.3 mm/s
- Bearing Temperature: 60–70°C (alarm at 85°C, trip at 95°C)
- Operating Pressure: 1.1–1.3 bar
- Motor Current: 43–47 A
- Operating Speed: 1470–1490 RPM

BEARING SPECIFICATIONS:
- Drive End: SKF 6315-2RS1 (Deep Groove Ball Bearing)
- Non-Drive End: SKF 6313-2RS1
- Lubrication: Shell Gadus S2 V220 2, 15g per point, monthly
- Bearing Change Interval: 18,000 hours or 2 years, whichever earlier

FAULT SIGNATURES AND DIAGNOSIS:
1. Bearing Wear (Most Common)
   Symptoms: Vibration > 3.5 mm/s, Temperature rise 5–10°C above baseline,
             High-frequency noise (metallic), RPM instability
   Cause: Inadequate lubrication, contamination, overloading
   Action: Vibration trending analysis, grease check, bearing inspection

2. Imbalance
   Symptoms: 1X vibration spike, vibration proportional to speed,
             No temperature change
   Cause: Blade erosion, material buildup on impeller
   Action: Dynamic balancing, blade inspection

3. Misalignment
   Symptoms: 2X vibration spike, axial vibration high,
             Coupling wear
   Cause: Thermal expansion, foundation shift
   Action: Laser alignment check

MAINTENANCE INTERVALS:
- Weekly: Visual inspection, lubrication check, vibration spot check
- Monthly: Full vibration measurement, oil analysis
- Quarterly: Coupling inspection, foundation bolt check
- Annual: Full overhaul, bearing replacement as needed
